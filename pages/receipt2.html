Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Boiled Water
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Chopped Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

How to do?

  1. Sauté the garlic and onion in olive oil.
  2. Add the rice and let it fry for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add boiling water and salt.
  4. Abaixe o fogo e deixe cozinhar até a água quase secar
  5. Cover the pan and wait about 20 minutes before serving.
  6. Now enjoy!
